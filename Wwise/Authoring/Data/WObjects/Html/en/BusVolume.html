Bus Volume

The attenuation (level or amplitude) applied on the audio signal at the bus or Auxiliary Bus level. Refer to Understanding the Voice Pipeline for more information about volumes.

Note: The default slider range is from -96 to +12. You can go over those limits by entering the value directly, or by sliding the mouse while the focus is on the edit control. The Vertical Fader is displayed for the Bus Volume setting by default. To switch to the Horizontal Slider, right-click the Volume setting and select Use Horizontal Slider for Volume.

Default value: 0
Range: -200 to 200
Units: dB
