3D Spatialization

Specifies whether the positioning of the source is calculated to simulate movement within a 3D space. Select from the following options:

  • None (default): The sound is panned according to the Speaker Panning setting.
  • Position: The sound is panned according to the relative positions of the emitter and listener.
  • Position + Orientation: The sound is panned according to the relative positions of the emitter and listener. The multichannel content of the sound also rotates with respect to the relative orientations of the emitter and listener. Orientation only has an effect with multichannel input files and a spread greater than zero.

Default value: None
