SetBusConfig and ResetBusConfig cannot modify master bus speaker configuration, it is dictated by the output device.

This error originates from AK::SoundEngine::SetBusConfig and indicates that the audioNodeID parameter points to a Bus that is a Main Audio Bus (top-most level in the hierarchy). Main Audio Busses take their speaker configuration from the hardware they output to.

推荐的解决步骤：

  • 仅提供子总线 ID。确认 Wwise 工程中的总线结构。

  • 检查总线名称/ID 有无拼写错误。
